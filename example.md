## Stop and Restart Instance

To restart the instance:

1.  Click the **Stop** button in the **Stop Instance** area.
    
    \[Image: Instructions for managing NavVis IVION instances including options to go, reset, stop, and archive.\]
    

A confirmation screen is displayed.

2.  Click **Stop Instance**.
    
3.  The instance will stop, wait a brief moment and the **Restart** button will display.
    
4.  Click the **Restart** button in the **Restart Instance** area.  
    \[Image: Button to restart the NavVis IVION instance with instructions above.\]
    

During the stop and restart process:

*   No data is lost.
    
*   Jobs processing in IVION Processing will not be affected.
    
*   Uploads/downloads will be terminated.
    
*   Jobs (e.g. map generation, etc.) will be terminated.

### FAQ

#### How do I access the Instance Management screen?

You can access the Instance Management screen by using your admin credentials to log into https://iv.navvis.com/.

#### What steps do I need to follow to reset the instance password?

To reset the instance password, click the Reset button in the Instance Management > Reset Instance Password area.

#### What happens when I stop and restart an instance?

When you stop and restart an instance, no data is lost, jobs processing in IVION Processing will not be affected, but uploads/downloads and jobs like map generation will be terminated.

#### Can I rename my instance, and will it change the URL?

Yes, you can rename your instance, but changing the instance name does not change the URL.

#### Where can I find information about my subscription details?

You can find information about your subscription details in the Subscription Details area, which includes your current plan and renewal date.

#### What information can I view in the Instance Information area?

In the Instance Information area, you can view the instance name, URL, geographical region, maximum area, maximum size in GB, and the status of the instance.

#### What does the status 'failed' mean in the instance status history?

The status 'failed' indicates that an error occurred during instance creation, and you should contact support.

#### Is the instance available for use when it shows 'online' status?

Yes, when the instance status is 'online', it is available for use.

#### What should I do if my instance subscription has expired?

If your instance subscription has expired, you will need to renew your plan to continue using the instance.
